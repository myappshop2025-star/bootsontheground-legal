By registering as a Field Agent on the Boots on the Ground Services LLC (BOTG) platform you acknowledge and agree that you are operating as an independent contractor only. You are not an employee, agent, partner, or representative of BOTG in any capacity. BOTG does not control your schedule, methods, or means of completing jobs.

All services performed through the BOTG platform are strictly limited to visual observation and photo or video documentation. You are not authorized to perform, represent, or imply that you are performing any licensed professional service of any kind including but not limited to:

  • Licensed home inspection
  • Structural assessment

  • Any other regulated professional service

You must never represent yourself to any client, property occupant, or third party as a licensed inspector, contractor, appraiser, or real estate professional in connection with any job performed through this platform.

You may only access areas of a property that are publicly accessible or for which you have received explicit written authorization from the client through the BOTG platform prior to beginning the job. BOTG assumes no liability for any legal consequences arising from unauthorized property access. You are solely responsible for ensuring you have proper authorization before accessing any non-public area of a property.

Your personal safety is your responsibility at all times while performing jobs through the BOTG platform. BOTG does not guarantee the availability of jobs in your area at any time. Job availability varies by location, time, and demand. BOTG makes no representations regarding the volume or frequency of jobs available to any individual Field Agent.

6. PLATFORM FEE ACKNOWLEDGMENT

You acknowledge and understand that BOTG deducts a platform fee of 10% of the job amount from your payout with a minimum deduction of $2.00 per job. This fee is deducted automatically before your payout is processed.

Payout timing: After client approval, your net earnings are transferred to your Stripe Connect balance when the client’s payment has cleared through Stripe—not necessarily on the same day you complete or receive approval for the job. Card payments typically settle within two (2) to seven (7) business days (longer for new accounts or ACH). Connect transfer usually completes within minutes after settlement; withdrawing to your bank is a separate step in the app. See the Field Agent Independent Contractor Agreement for full payout terms.

You are solely responsible for reporting all income earned through the BOTG platform to the appropriate federal and state tax authorities. BOTG will issue a 1099-NEC form where required by law. BOTG does not withhold taxes from your payouts.

You may not directly solicit, contact, or conduct business with any client you connect with through the BOTG platform outside of the platform. All jobs, communications, and payments must be conducted exclusively through the platform. Violation of this policy may result in immediate account termination and a penalty fee of $500 per occurrence.
